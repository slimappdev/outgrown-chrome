Privacy Policy for Product Info Extractor

Last updated: 10/27/2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Product Info Extractor ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Data Collection

We collect the following data only when you explicitly click the "Extract Product Info" button:

  • Product titles
  • Product prices
  • Product descriptions
  • Product page URLs

For Google Sheets integration, we access:

  • OAuth access token for Google Sheets
  • Spreadsheet ID (stored locally)

3. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is:

  • Only stored in your personal Google Spreadsheet
  • Not shared with any third parties
  • Not used for any marketing or advertising purposes
  • Only processed when you explicitly initiate the extraction

4. Data Storage

Our data storage practices:

  • Product data is stored exclusively in your Google Sheets account
  • Only the spreadsheet ID is stored locally in the extension
  • No personal data is stored within the extension

5. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Access your data anytime through your Google Spreadsheet
  • Delete any or all extracted data from your spreadsheet
  • Revoke the extension's access to Google Sheets at any time
  • Clear the extension's local storage through Chrome's settings

6. Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To read product information from your current tab when you click the extension button
  • storage: To save your spreadsheet ID locally
  • identity: For secure Google Sheets authentication
  • sheets.googleapis.com: To communicate with Google Sheets API

7. Data Security

We protect your data by:

  • Using secure Google OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Not storing any sensitive information locally
  • Only accessing data when explicitly requested
  • Using HTTPS for all API communications

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the Chrome Web Store and within the extension.

9. Contact Information

For any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at slimappdev@gmail.com.
